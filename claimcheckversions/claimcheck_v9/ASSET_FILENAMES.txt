ClaimCheck AU asset naming guide
================================

Brand logo next to company name
-------------------------------
assets/brand/claimcheck-logo.png

Browser tab icon, optional
--------------------------
assets/brand/favicon.png

Hero banner images
------------------
Put your 10 banner images here. The website creates the top navigation bar itself, so export the banner artwork WITHOUT the top bar/logo/nav/phone line baked into the image.

Recommended size: 1916 x 821 px or 1920 x 820 px.
Leave enough empty/dark space at the top of the image because the website header sits over the top area.
